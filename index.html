Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Our professional technicians typically complete installation within 48 hours of your request. This includes router setup, network configuration, and testing to ensure optimal performance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. Monthly subscriptions can be paid through M-Pesa for convenience.

Is there a data limit on packages?

All our packages offer unlimited data usage. You only pay the monthly subscription fee with no additional charges for data consumption.

What if I experience connectivity issues?

Contact our 24/7 support team immediately. We provide remote troubleshooting and can dispatch a technician if needed, usually within 4 hours during business days.

Can I upgrade my package anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade your package at any time. The new speed will be activated within 24 hours, and billing adjustments will be prorated.

Do you provide the router?

Yes, a high-quality WiFi router is included in the installation package. We also provide ongoing technical support and replacement if needed.
